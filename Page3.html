3) Instant Money Order (iMO)
The instant domestic Money Transfer Service
India Post presents Instant Money Order (iMO), the instant on-line money transfer service that is instant, convenient,reliable and affordable.iMO is an instant web based money transfer service through Post Offices (iMO Centre) in India between two resident individuals in Indian territory. You can transfer money from INR 1,000/- to INR 50,000/- from designated iMO Post Offices.It is simple to send and receive money.
